## 1. Getting Started
### 🔑 Login
- Navigate to the system URL.
- Enter your registered email and password.
- Default Email: [demoapp@samrtgym.com]`
- Default Password: `[demo@1234]`
- You must **Accept the Terms & Conditions** to proceed to the dashboard.

### ⚙️ Initial Setup
- If you are a new gym owner, you will be prompted to set up your gym profile.
- Enter your Gym Name, Location, and select your subscription plan.
- Upload your Gym Logo for personalized branding.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is your "Control Center." It provides a real-time snapshot of your business health.
- **Active Members:** Count of members with currently valid subscriptions.
- **Monthly Revenue:** Total income collected in the current calendar month.
- **Pending Payments:** Number of members whose subscriptions have expired or are due soon.
- **Attendance Heatmap:** A visual chart showing peak hours in your gym.

---

## 3. Member Management
### ➕ Adding a Member
- Go to the **Members** page.
- Click **Add New Member**.
- Fill in details: Name, Email, Phone, and select a Membership Plan.
- A unique **QR Code** is automatically generated for every member.

### 👤 Member Profiles
- Click on any member to see their full history.
- View their attendance logs, payment records, and subscription status.
- You can manually freeze or deactivate a member's account from this view.

---

## 4. Scanner & Attendance
The scanner is designed to be used at the gym entrance.
- **Camera Access:** Ensure your browser has permission to access the webcam.
- **Scanning:** Point the member's QR code toward the camera.
- **Modes:**
    - **Lens Mode:** Quick preview of the member's status.
    - **Attendance Mode:** Automatically logs the visit and displays a "Check-in Successful" message.
- **Validation:** The system will flag members with expired subscriptions in **RED** and block their check-in.

---

## 5. Payments & Billing
### 💰 Tracking Revenue
- The **Payments** page lists all transactions.
- You can filter payments by date, member, or status (Paid/Pending).
- Generate PDF receipts for members directly from the payment detail view.

### 📊 Accounting
- Use the **Accounting** tab to view financial growth charts.
- Export financial data as CSV for tax or external accounting purposes.

---

## 6. Retention & Churn Watchlist
*This is the system's most powerful feature.*
- **Churn Watchlist:** Lists members who haven't visited the gym in over 7 days.
- **Risk Levels:** Members are flagged as "Low Risk," "Medium Risk," or "High Risk" based on their attendance drop-off.
- **Action:** Click "Contact" next to a high-risk member to send a pre-filled reminder or offer to bring them back.

---

## 7. Notifications & Reminders
### 🔔 Automated Alerts
- **Payment Reminders:** The system automatically sends notifications 3 days before a subscription expires.
- **System Announcements:** Broadcast messages to all members via the Member Portal.
