🏢 Smart Gym Management & Retention System (MERN)
📌 Executive Summary

The Smart Gym Management & Retention System is a full-stack, real-time SaaS-style web application designed to help gym owners manage operations, improve member engagement, and reduce churn.

Unlike traditional systems, it integrates:

Operational management
Retention intelligence
Rule enforcement (fine/ban)
User engagement (gamification + offers)
Real-time system updates
🎯 Problem Definition

Gym businesses struggle with:

High member churn
Lack of engagement visibility
Manual record systems
No automated retention strategies
No real-time operational awareness

👉 Result: Revenue loss + inefficient management

💡 Solution Vision

A smart, data-driven gym platform that:

Automates operations
Tracks user behavior
Enforces rules
Engages users
Enables real-time decision-making
